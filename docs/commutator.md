
# Commutator
## Definition
$$
[g,h]_R = g^{-1} h^{-1}gh
$$
$$
[g,h]_L = ghg^{-1} h^{-1}
$$
$gh$ is on right side for right commutator.  
In most case, whether the commutator $[g,h]$ is R or L is depend on the context.  
**We use R commutator in this project**
## Property
$$
[g,h] = [h,g]^{-1}
$$
$$
gh=[g,h]_Lhg=hg[h,g]_R
$$

$$
\text{If } [g,h] \text{ is Abelian, then } [g,h]_R = [g,h]_L
$$



# Conjugation
$$
g^h=h^{-1}gh
$$
$$
^hg=hgh^{-1}
$$
